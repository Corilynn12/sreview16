Indian Flat Bread

Sangeetha Maheshwari

Main Ingredients: Two cups, wheat flour Two tablespoons, oil Salt, to taste ¾ cup, water

Other ingredients: Wheat Flour, a little extra on side for rolling the flat bread Oil, to lightly fry the flat bread Butter, a dollop to serve on top of flat bread (optional)

Preparing the dough: Mix whole wheat flour, salt and oil in a medium bowl. Mix well. Start adding little water at a time and knead into soft and smooth dough. Cover it and let it rest for 15 minutes.

Making the flat bread: Knead the dough to smooth it out and divide it into eight equal portions. Using your palm, make a smooth ball of the dough and flatten it out. Take one flattened ball, dip it into dry flour and roll into a circle with a six to eight inch diameter, using a rolling pin. Use more dry flour as needed to make rolling easy. Heat the non-stick pan on medium heat. Once hot, place the rolled out dough and cook for six to eight seconds on each side. Put ¼ teaspoon of oil, spread it all over the bread using a spoon. Fry one side and then the other using a spatula. Remove from pan and serve hot. Repeat the same process for rest of the bread. Serve with vegetable, meats or rice.
